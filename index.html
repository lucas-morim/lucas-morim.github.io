Who I Am

I'm a Multimedia Engineering student at ISLA Gaia, Porto, with hands-on professional experience shipping production software at real scale. From developing large-scale financial applications at BTG Pactual in São Paulo to engineering enterprise internal tools at Capgemini Engineering in Portugal — I write code that people depend on.

My focus is .NET and C# on the backend — building clean, well-tested APIs with Entity Framework and SQL Server. On the frontend, I pair that with Angular. I've worked with other stacks along the way (Node.js, Laravel, Python), but .NET is where I'm doubling down.

I've also taught on the other side of the desk — as a Lecturer at ISTEC, covering Algorithms and Data Structures in C#. Teaching fundamentals sharpened how I think about software, not just how to use frameworks.
